# Decision Log

Record **architectural and design decisions** here once made. Don't re-litigate decisions that live here — amend the entry if the call changes.

**What belongs here:** design choices, tradeoffs, implementation decisions — things that are made and settled.

**What does NOT belong here:** plans, timelines, future directions, to-do items, or anything that is a *plan* rather than a *decision*.

---

## Template

### [YYYY-MM-DD] Decision title

**Decision:** What was decided.

**Alternatives considered:**
- Option A — why it was rejected
- Option B — why it was rejected

**Chosen:** Which option and the key reason.

**Rationale:** The constraint, deadline, or principle that drove the choice.
